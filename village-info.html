About Our Village

Discover the rich history, culture, and facilities of our beautiful Gram Panchayat.

History & Culture

Village name is Kaithi, a beautiful village with around 250 houses and 1200 house holds living freely in fresh air and moving towards advancement, holding a history of aprox 200 years, containing different viewable areas, a beautiful temple and lots of greenery.

Schools

We have a Government Primary School and a Senior Secondary School providing quality education to over 800 children.

Healthcare

The Primary Health Center (PHC) is equipped with basic emergency services, a pharmacy, and visiting doctors.
